Grand Bliss Sea, Bentota


Welcome to Grand Bliss Sea Bentota, where the calming seas of the Indian ocean meets the timeless elegance of comfortable and relaxed living. Nestled on the pristine beaches of Bentota, Sri Lanka, our resort invites you to immerse yourself in a world of tranquility, and natural beauty.

Grand Bliss Sea, Bentota

We are located in the midst of famous tourist attractions such as a scenic stretch of soft sand with lush foliage, an electric evening skyline with sunset in the south coast of Sri Lanka. Guests have convenient access to outdoor activities such as Jet Skiing, tube rides, boat rides, windsurfing, water boarding and many more!

Grand Bliss Lake, Habarana


Set on the picturesque and forested land bordering the tranquil lake, Grand Bliss Lake Habarana offers a unique and peaceful retreat. Wake up to the symphony of birdsong and the mild rustling of leaves, creating an atmosphere of pure serenity.

Grand Bliss Lake, Habarana

Enjoy and experience popular attractions such as the historic Dambulla Cave Temple, Elephant Safari at the Minneriya National Park, home for the largest elephant gathering in the world during July to October, and a stunning ecosystem which is home to nearly 50 different birds and kinds of butterflies, acknowledged by our guests.
